Possible sign of phishing:
  To field is blank
  Unusual email client
  Suspicious path from sender to recipient
    Claims to be sent from US, but originates from China 
  Mismatch in sender address
    smtp.mailfrom
    Return-Path
    From:
    Reply-to/Bounces-to
  Failed DKIM/SPF verification
